Our Services

At Schelling Overhead Doors, we pride ourselves on offering a comprehensive range of services to meet all your garage door needs. Our team of skilled technicians is dedicated to providing top-notch service, whether you need a new installation, routine maintenance, or emergency repairs.

Our Services Include:

  • Garage Door Installation: We offer professional installation services for a wide variety of garage doors, ensuring a perfect fit and smooth operation.
  • Repair Services: From broken springs to malfunctioning openers, our technicians can quickly diagnose and fix any issues with your garage door.
  • Maintenance Plans: Regular maintenance is key to extending the life of your garage door. We offer customized maintenance plans to keep your door in optimal condition.
  • Emergency Services: Garage door problems can happen at any time. That's why we provide 24/7 emergency repair services to get your door back up and running as soon as possible.
