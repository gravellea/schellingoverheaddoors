Frequently Asked Questions

Should I replace my whole garage door system or just the panels?

+

The decision depends on several factors. If your door is over 15 years old or the track system is damaged, a full replacement is recommended. However, if only the panels are damaged and your door system is relatively new, panel replacement might be more cost-effective.

How often should I service my garage door?

+

We recommend professional servicing at least once a year to ensure safe and reliable operation. However, if you use your door frequently or notice any unusual sounds or movements, more frequent inspections may be necessary.

What maintenance can I do myself?

+

You can perform basic maintenance like visual inspections, cleaning tracks, and lubricating moving parts. However, leave spring adjustments and electrical work to professionals as these can be dangerous.

How long does installation typically take?

+

A standard garage door installation typically takes 4-6 hours. However, custom installations or removing old doors may require additional time. We'll provide a specific timeframe during your consultation.

Do you offer warranty coverage?

+

Yes, we offer comprehensive warranty coverage on both parts and labor. Our doors come with manufacturer warranties, and we provide additional coverage on our installation work. Specific terms vary by product.
